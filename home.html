  • •Track Management

      import and view external data tracks

  • •BLAST

      The Basic Local Alignment Search Tool (BLAST) finds regions of local similarity between sequences.

  • •Plant Genomes

      View the species relationship of multiple subspecies of poplar and similar plants. Users can view the visualized genomic data of the comparison in the visualization panel of the browser.

About Us

At the end of 2019, Professor Guohua Wang from Northeast Forestry University leads the Bioinformatics Laboratory started a project to build a poplar genome browser.
At the end of 2020, the Bioinformatics Laboratory of Northeast Forestry University completed the first version of the poplar genome browser.
